About Market Mate

Market Mate helps mama mboga (vegetable sellers) track stock, sales and profits easily.

Problems Solved

  • Track what you buy and sell daily
  • Know exactly how much profit you make
  • Never forget what stock you have

Features

  • Add vegetables with buy/sell prices
  • Record sales instantly
  • Dashboard shows totals automatically
  • Saves data in your browser (no internet needed)
